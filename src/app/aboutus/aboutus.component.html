Learn About The Founders of SeaGames

SeaGames was founded by Abdullah Jandali and William Huang.

William Huang

William Huang is a cool dude who's very patient and understanding, leading the charge with making SeaGame's website.
He wants to work in the video game industry when he graduates. Is he open to working in other areas of programming? Sure, but being a videogame developer has been his dream for a while now. He enjoys watching anime. Growing up, he played games like Roblox, Adventure Quest Worlds, FusionFall, and Ninja Saga.

Abdullah Jandali

Despite being a bit slow, Abdullah Jandali is always churning out ideas for something or other. Once he puts his mind to his work, there's no stopping him.
